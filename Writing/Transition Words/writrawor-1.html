Greek yogurt is slightly lower in carbohydrates that regular yogurt. Also, because it is more concentrated, Greek yogurt contains slightly more protein per serving, thereby helping people stay satiated for longer periods of time.

Explanation

Also is the correct transition word because it describes an additional statement that is similar to the one before it. However, the statements can be placed in either order.

"Consequently", "For example,", and "In other words," imply a relationship between the two statements that requires the second statement to follow the first.


Here are some examples of when we would use the other answer choices:

Greek yogurt is slightly lower in carbohydrates that regular yogurt. Consequently, individuals will need to fulfill their carbohydrate needs with other foods.

Greek yogurt is slightly lower in carbohydrates that regular yogurt. For example, Axle Greek Yogurt has 12 grams of carbs per serving compared with Bob's Yogurt, which has 25 grams of carbs per serving.

Greek yogurt is slightly lower in carbohydrates that regular yogurt. In other words, it is great for diabetics looking to control their sugar consumption.

If you tried to reverse the order of the sentences, the excerpts would no longer read properly.
