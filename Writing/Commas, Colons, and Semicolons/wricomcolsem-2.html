Explanation

The overall structure of the sentence is as follows:

$$ \underbrace{\text{Because families...}}_\text{dependent clause},\underbrace{\text{...should continue developing better methods...}}_\text{independent clause} $$

Conjunctions such as "therefore" and "so" are unnecessary due to "Because".

Colons can be used between two independent clauses, but not after a dependent clause.

A comma here is the best choice for combining a dependent and independent clause.
