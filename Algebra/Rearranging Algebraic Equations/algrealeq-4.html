During a 8-second time interval, the average acceleration \(a\), in meters per second squared, of an object with a initial velocity of 5 meters per second is defined by the equation \(a=\dfrac{v_f-5}{8} \), where \(v_f\) is the final velocity of the object in meters per second. If the equation is rewritten in the form \(v_f=xa+y\), where \(x\) and \(y\) are constants, what is the value of \(y\)?

Approach
