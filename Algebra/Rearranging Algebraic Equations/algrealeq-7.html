The average velocity \(v\) of an object is found by dividing the distance \(d\) of an object by its time \(t\). Which fo the following equations gives the time \(t\) in terms of \(v\) and \(d\)?

Approach

Given the initial discription,

$$ v=\frac{d}{t} $$

Solving for \(t\):

$$ tv=d $$ $$ \boxed{t=\frac{d}{v}} $$
