If \( \sqrt[4]{x^3} \cdot \sqrt[3]{x^2} = x^a \) for all values of \(x\), what is the value of \(a\)?

Choose 1 Answer:

Approach

Simplification is easier when the exponents are written in fractional form:

$$ \sqrt[4]{x^3} \cdot \sqrt[3]{x^2} = x^{\frac{3}{4}} \cdot x^{\frac{2}{3}}$$ $$ = x^{\frac{3\cdot 2}{4 \cdot 3}} $$ $$ =x^{\frac{6}{12}} $$ $$ = x^{\frac{1}{2}} $$
