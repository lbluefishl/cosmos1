The budget for a school club was $1,000 in 2020. The budget decreased by 12% from 2020 to 2021 and then increased by 18% from 2021 to 2022. Which of the following expressions represents the budget, in dollars, for the school in 2022?

Choose 1 Answer:

Approach

A review of percent increase and decrease:

12% decrease
$$ 1{,}000(1)-1{,}000\cdot(0.12) $$ $$ = 1{,}000(0.88) $$
18% increase
$$ 1{,}000(1)+1{,}000\cdot(0.18) $$ $$ = 1{,}000(1.18) $$

Since percents are multiplicative, it doesn't matter which order we apply the percents.

$$ \boxed{1{,}000(0.88)(1.18)} $$
