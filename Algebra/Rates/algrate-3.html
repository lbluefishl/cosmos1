Professional video game players typically have high APM. APM refers to the number of actions they make per minute. Actions refer to keystrokes and mouse clicks. If a certain player makes 3,150 mouse clicks and 10,215 keyboard clicks over the course of a 2 hour 15 minute game, what was the player's APM for the game?

Choose 1 Answer:

Approach

We want to find the number of actions the player makes per minute.

$$ \text{APM} =\frac{\text{ Actions}}{\text{Minute}}$$ $$\text{Actions} = 3{,}150+10{,}215 = 13{,}365$$ $$\text{Minutes}=\text{2 hours 15 minutes}=\text{135 minutes} $$ $$ \text{APM}= \frac{13{,}365}{135} $$ $$ \text{APM}=\boxed{99} $$
