What value of \(x\) satisfies the following equation? $$ 2(5x-20)-(25-8x)=7 $$

Choose 1 Answer:

Approach 1

$$ 2(5x-20)-(25-8x)=7 $$ $$ 10x-40-25+8x=7 $$ $$ 18x-65=7 $$ $$ 18x=72 $$ $$x=4 $$
