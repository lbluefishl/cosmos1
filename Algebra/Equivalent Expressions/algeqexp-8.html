We want to expand and convert the polynomial into its factored form.

$$(x^3+6x^2)-(5x^2+12x) $$ $$ =x^3+6x^2-5x^2-12x $$ $$ =x^3+x^2-12x $$ $$ =x(x^2+x-12) $$ $$ =x(x+4)(x-3) $$

$$a=0, b=4, c=-3 $$ $$ a+b+c= $$ $$ 0+4-3=\boxed{1}$$
